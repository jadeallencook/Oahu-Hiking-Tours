Experience Oahu

Oahu is comprised of two major mountain ranges, the Ko'alaus and the Waianae Mountains; the majority of these trails are overgrown, rugged, and challenging hikes. Your guide will lead you through the jungled wilderness, along jagged cliffs, up and down summits, then back safely to your rental car.

Guided Hikes

Every one of our hikes is guided from start to finish. You will receive directions to the trail head of choice. And you will be briefed specifically for the chosen trails terrain, elevation, and time allotment.

Photo Opportunities

Oahu hikes are filled with spectacular views. Be sure to bring extra batteries and memory cards. Any waterproof gear, casing, or bags will absolutely prove its allegiance during your trip.

Worry Free

Our guides are experienced, trained and Red Cross certified in CPR, as well as first aid. We know what supplies and gear you need for each and every hike. Let Oahu Hiking Tours lead you on your journey, safely and worry free.

Rain Or Shine

Hikes will be completed regardless of weather, unless the safety of hikers is compromised. Some trails allow for flash flooding, landslides, or rock falls under severe weather circumstances. Conditions will vary between tours.
